How It Works

A seamless, native three-step process built directly into your Google Gemini interface.

1. Highlight Any Text

Select any portion of a Gemini response. A floating "Ask Gemini" button immediately appears right above your selection.

Here is the detailed project summary you asked for. The budget has been set to $10,000 for phase one.
Ask Gemini

2. Lock in the Context

Clicking the button pins that text to your prompt bar as an elegant context chip. Now your follow-up is locked onto that reference.

Replying to: "$10,000 for phase one"
Can we divide this across three milestones?

3. Reply Cleanly

Your follow-up is processed with the quote embedded. In your chat history, the technical details are hidden, replaced by a beautiful context preview block.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Quote Reply for Gemini.

Is this extension free? +
Yes, Quote Reply is 100% free to use. There are no paid tiers, ads, or premium subscriptions.
How does it protect my privacy? +
All text processing occurs locally on your browser. Your conversations and highlighted quotes are never sent to our servers. Optional, anonymous usage telemetry is used to help improve the extension and can be toggled off at any time.
Does it work on other websites? +
No, this extension is custom-built specifically for Google Gemini (gemini.google.com) to provide a deeply integrated, native-feeling user experience.
How do I access settings or view my stats? +
Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar. A clean dashboard popup will open showing your usage stats, time saved, and developer mode/telemetry toggles.
Does this work with Gemini Advanced/Workspace? +
Yes, the extension supports all Gemini tiers, including Gemini Advanced, Workspace versions, and dark/light themes.
