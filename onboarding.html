Quote Reply for Gemini

How to use Quote Reply

1

Highlight target text

Select any text in Gemini's response that you want to reply to.

2

Click "Ask Gemini"

A floating button will pop up near your cursor. Click it to lock in your context.

3

Type & Send

Enter your follow-up reply in the text area and press enter. Your highlighted context is sent with it.
