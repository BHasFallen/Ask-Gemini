Powerbox for Gemini

How to use Powerbox

1

Quote Reply

Select any text in Gemini's response, click the floating "Ask Gemini" button, and type your reply.

2

Usage Limits Tracker

Gemini Advanced users will see their usage metrics directly in the left-hand sidebar.

3

Export Chats to PDF

Click the "Export PDF" button next to any chat session header to print and save your logs.
